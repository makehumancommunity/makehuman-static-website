MakeHuman 2 is a free, open-source tool for quickly creating realistic 3D human characters through a simple
point-and-click interface. Like its predecessor, it lets you model characters by manipulating controls for
attributes such as gender, age, height, weight and ethnicity, as well as low-level details such as the shape
of the eyes, mouth or fingers. The finished character can then be exported for use in other applications.

It is a complete rewrite and the successor of MakeHuman 1, which has been running for more than a decade. The
rewrite brings a modern foundation (PySide6/Qt6 and OpenGL) along with new capabilities such as additional base
meshes, skeletal animation, physically-based rendering (PBR) and glTF export.

> **Note:** MakeHuman 2 is currently in alpha. It can be installed and run in parallel with MakeHuman 1, so you
> can try it without giving up your existing setup.
